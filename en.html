A warm welcome

in our bright and stylishly furnished holiday home in the heart of Porto Cristo. The flat is situated on the first floor of a well-maintained two-family house in a quiet side street and offers the perfect combination of a central location and a relaxed atmosphere.

The fine sandy beach, the picturesque fishing harbour, restaurants, cafés and shops are all just a few minutes’ walk away. There is usually plenty of public parking available right outside the house.

The apartment has two bedrooms, two modern bathrooms – one of which is en suite – and a spacious, light-filled living room.

The separate kitchen is fully equipped. There is also a utility room with a washing machine.

The real highlight is the large private sun terrace. It offers plenty of space to relax, sunbathe or enjoy cosy evenings outdoors. The outdoor area is complemented by a balcony with a seating area.

Several air conditioning units with cooling and heating functions ensure pleasant temperatures all year round. Large fitted wardrobes provide plenty of storage space. The flat is ideal for two adults and, on request, a small pet.
